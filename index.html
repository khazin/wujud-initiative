About Us

Formerly known as 'Voyage of Knowledge', we started as a Ground Up Initiative in National University Singapore (NUS) in 2015 with the help of the NUS Muslim Society to provide Islamic Classes with the theme of the intellectual diversity that can raise inspiration and awareness from our predecessors in faith.

Today, we aim to provide them to everyone to benefit from it.

Our Mission

To inspire and make aware of the diversity and heritage left behind by our predecessors in faith

our vision

To promote intellectual discourse in Islam.

Programmes

Wujud Initiative arose to meet the needs of Muslims who are looking for an intelllectual appreciation of their faith.

Our classes cuts across a range of topics, from Islamic intellectual history to ethics and philosophy, theology, Sufism, and contemporary thought. Browse through all past and upcoming classes offered here at Wujud Initiative. Through our signature events and classes, we hope to satisfy the desired level of discourse on Islam many are seeking.

    17th November 2024, 11:00 am - 12:30 pm

    Book Sharing by Dr. Khairudin Aljunied - Contemplating Sufism: Dialogue and Tradition across Southeast Asia

    Come join us for a sharing on this upcoming book by Dr Khairudin Aljunied! Contemplating Sufism: Dialogue and Tradition across Southeast Asia is an upcoming book by Khairudin Aljunied that explores the factors and forces that have enabled Islam to assert and embed itself in Southeast Asia. Using a unique “contemplative histories” methodology, Khairudin Aljunied reveals the multiple undercurrents that continue to influence Sufi thought and practices.

    Khairudin Aljunied

    Khairudin Aljunied is an Associate Professor at the National University of Singapore (NUS) and a Senior Fellow at the Alwaleed Bin Talal Center for Muslim-Christian Understanding at Georgetown University and . He has held visiting professorships at Columbia University, the University of Brunei Darussalam, and the University of Malaya.
